About Us:

At Váltók, we are a creative Pan Africanist tech company based in Kigali, Rwanda, driven by a passion for leveraging technology to empower Africa. Our team, consisting of ISHEMA Toussaint, Isheja La Douce, and Nshuti Guillaume, brings a wealth of experience and expertise in web development, graphic design, and videography.

Our Mission:

To provide innovative tech solutions that transform businesses, organizations, and communities across Africa, fostering growth, and driving positive change.

Why Choose Us?
